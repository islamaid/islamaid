About Us

Islamaid is a registered charity working in Africa to support Muslim families, communities, and vulnerable individuals. We provide food and water during fasting seasons, offer medical aid to those affected by HIV/AIDS, build mosques, respond to disasters, and promote transparency, accountability, and inclusion in all our activities.

Our Work

  • Food & Drink Support during Ramadan and Sitat Fast
  • Animal Slaughter for Communities
  • Water Projects: Wells and Clean Water Sources
  • Mosque Construction
  • HIV/AIDS Medical Support and Clinics
  • Emergency Relief Aid
